SLIDE 33
Profiling Engine
3.Profiling Engine : Profile - Student Score Calculator 
Step 1: 
Map all the competencies taught for a project to profiles and determine the profiles that gets unlocked for that project- at least 2 primary competencies to be listed to unlock a profile
Step 3: 
Do student level scouting to identify each students top 5-6 competencies from project and convert to scale to calculate.
Step 4: 
Based on primary competencies shortlist the top 5 possible profiles.(all primary competency wrt to project need to be present to shortlist)
Step 5: 
Do total score calculation including primary and secondary competencies. Identify the top 3 profiles and the common strengths
Profile Detail for Student calculated and reported at project level. Reset after every project 
Step 2: Distribute 100% weightage across the competencies present. All secondary competencies weighted at 10%. The pending distributed across primary.
Stage 3- Skill passport stage disabled in CSL+
